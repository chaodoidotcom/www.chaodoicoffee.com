About CHAO DOI Coffee

Aroma group is a Thai company that has been in the coffee industry for more than 60 years and pays special attention in selecting the right kind of coffee beans for Thai people. They also intend to share their knowledge and experience of coffee to others. 


So anyone who is interested in owning a Chao Doi coffee shop is able to do so under a franchise business with Aroma group.  Any new coffee shop investor of Thai Nationality can chose the Chao Doi Coffee brand as a new option to open their branch.
